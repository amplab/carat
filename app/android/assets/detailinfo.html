What are these numbers?

Expected benefit is the amount of battery life that fixing the hog/bug is expected to yield. Fixing hogs means killing the app and using it less, while fixing a bug may mean just restarting the app, updating it to the latest version, or changing some of the app's settings.

Samples shows how many measurements we have of this app, across all users. More samples means the app is common and its energy use well known.

As there are more samples, the Measurement error (in minutes) will decrease.

Measurements without shows how many measurements Carat has that do not include the app in question. Both measurements with and without the app are required to ascertain its energy behavior.
